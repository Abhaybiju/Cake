ABOUT US

Welcome to My Cakes, where every creation is baked with passion, love, and a sprinkle of happiness.

Welcome to my cakes

where every bite is a celebration!

Founded and lovingly run by Shijil, My Cakes is your neighborhood cake shop dedicated to creating delicious memories one slice at a time. What started as a passion for baking has grown into a warm, welcoming space where creativity meets flavor.

At My Cakes

We believe that every cake tells a story — whether it's a birthday, wedding, anniversary, or just because. From classic flavors to custom creations, each of our cakes is handmade with care, using only the finest ingredients to ensure freshness, taste, and a touch of magic in every bite

We're not just about cakes — we're about moments, milestones, and making people smile. Whether you're picking up a quick treat or planning the perfect cake for your special event, we're here to make it unforgettable.

Thank you for choosing My Cakes. We're honored to be a part of your sweetest moments.

With love,
Shijil & the My Cakes Team

Founder Speaks

Hi, I'm Shijil, the founder of My Cakes.

Baking has always been more than just a hobby for me — it's been a passion, a form of expression, and a way to bring joy to others. That passion led me to start My Cakes, a place where every cake is made with love, care, and the belief that even the simplest treat can make someone's day special.

My goal with My Cakes is to offer more than just beautifully baked goods — I want to create experiences. Whether it's a birthday, a wedding, a small celebration, or just a sweet craving, I'm honored to be part of those moments.

Thank you for visiting My Cakes. Your support means the world to me, and I promise to keep baking happiness into every bite.

With Gratitude
Shijil
